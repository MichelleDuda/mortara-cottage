About the Space

This beautiful cottage tucked away in Irish countryside is for adults only - not equipped for children or babies. It is an open plan space on the ground floor with a kitchen, dining and lounge space. The main bathroom is on ground floor. Our second floor consists of a Master Bedroom with king size bed and open plan dressing area. It also includes a second bathroom with shower. Guests also have access to an private outdoor area and a covered seating area.


The cottage is a blend of the old and the new. It is a luxurious space where you can enjoy comfort, peace, and tranquility.
